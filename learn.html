Welcome to Mera Kaam Meri Pahachan

Mera Kaam Meri Pehchan is a programme mainly aimed at providing training and employment to the women in rural areas.

Rural Women Employment

We are here to create RURAL WOMEN EMPLOYMENT by adding new skills and new ways of working in women's life. After this, they need not to work in different households or any agriculture land labour works. They will be self employed and there will be no barrier to stop them using this user-friendly platform. Here, we are guiding each and every individual, whosoever interested in being self employed or self dependent. We are helping humans (especially women and children) to develop new skills in their life, which will help them creating or fulfilling the great vision of our honourable prime minister i.e., AATMANIRBHAR BHARAT.
